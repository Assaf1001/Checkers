CHECKERS 2 PLAYERS

RULES

OBJECT

Remove all your opponent's checkers from the game board by capturing them.

HOW TO PLAY

White player to go first. On your turn, move any one of your checkers by the movement rules described below. After you move one checker, your turn is over. The game continues with players alternating turns.

Movement Rules

  • Always move your checker diagonally forward, toward your opponent's side of the gameboard
    Note: After checker becomes a "King", it can diagonally forward of backward across the gameboard.
  • Move your checker one space diagonally, to an open adjacent square, or jump one or more checkers diagonally to an open square adjacent to the checker you jumped. When you jump over an opponent's checker, you capture it (see Capturing an Opponent's Checker).
  • If all squares adjacent to your checker are occupied, your checker is blocked and cannot move

Capturing an Opponent's Checker

If your jump an opponent's checker, you capture it. The checker will be removed from the gameboard.
If you have an opportunity to capture you must do it.
You can jump over two or more checkers if you have the option, After the first jump you can jump backward.

Becoming a "King"

As soon as one of your checkers reaches the first row on opponent's side of the gameboard, its becomes a King. The checker will become golden and will be wearing a crown.

HOW TO WIN

The first player to capture all opposing checkers from the gameboard wins the game!
